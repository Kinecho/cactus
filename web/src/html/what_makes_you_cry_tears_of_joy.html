What makes you cry tears of joy?

Inspiration

"Tears of joy can be expressed when we share in the happiness of others. Crying is a way by which we express our empathic connection to others when they are celebrating wonderful occasions, which may also elicit our own personal joyful memories." Read more

Shoba Sreenivasan, Ph.D.
& Linda E. Weinberger, Ph.D.

Mindfulness

Remember a moment when you cried tears of joy. What made the moment meaningful to you? Visualize yourself in that moment once again. Allow the emotion to return. Express your gratitude to it.

Intention

How might we share in the happiness, and express our empathetic connection to others today?
