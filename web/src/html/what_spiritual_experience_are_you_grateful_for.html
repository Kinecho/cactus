What spiritual experience are you grateful for?

Inspiration

“Spirituality is not about looking up or down—it's about looking inward.”

Jaggi Vasudev

Spirituality is not tied to religion, though religion may be spiritual. Spirituality is the quality of being concerned with the human spirit or soul as opposed to material or physical things. Spirituality is where purpose, meaning, and presence meet.

Mindfulness

What made your spiritual experience meaningful? Visualize yourself in that moment. Express your gratitude to the moment, for the meaning it brought into your life.

Intention

Be observant of the experiences which allow you to be present, and express your gratitude towards them.
