Mindfulness

Take three deep breaths, inhaling through your nose, and exhaling slowly through your mouth, make a “whoosh” sound as you exhale. Visualize yourself doing your favorite renewal activity, replaying the experience of each of your senses. Feel the stress melt away, as you are filled with renewed energy and calm, as if being filled with water. Take three additional deep breaths, inhaling through your nose, and exhaling slowly through your mouth, making a “whoosh” sound as you exhale.
