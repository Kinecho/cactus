Mindfulness

Take three deep breaths. Visualize yourself in the place you feel most relaxed. Replay your senses: What do you hear? What do you see? What do you smell? What do you feel? What do you taste? Observe with curiosity how your body and mind react. Express your gratitude to your body/mind connection.

Intention

Embrace the body/mind connection with wonder and gratitude. Embrace how exercise and food can enhance our thoughts and emotions. Embrace how our thoughts and emotions create physical sensation. Marvel at the miracle of the ecosystem that is our mind and body. Treat yourself with kindness today.
